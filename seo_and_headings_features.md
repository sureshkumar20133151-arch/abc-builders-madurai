### 3. Dynamic XML Sitemap & Robots.txt
* **Sitemap (`/sitemap.xml`)**: Automatically generates a list of all your website pages (including both English `/en/...` and Tamil `/ta/...` versions) so Google can discover and index them instantly.
* **Robots (`/robots.txt`)**: Commands search engine spiders on how to crawl the site and points them directly to your sitemap.

### 4. Semantic Heading Hierarchy (Outline Audit)
* We audited all pages and restructured HTML headings (`<h1>`, `<h2>`, `<h3>`) to follow a strict hierarchy.
* Each page has **exactly one `H1`** tag (main page title).
* Subsections use `H2` for main categories (e.g., "Our Core Services") and `H3` for specific points (e.g., "CMDA Permit"), with no skipped levels.
* This makes the site highly accessible for screen readers and vastly improves Google's understanding of the site structure.
